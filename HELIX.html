HELIX

Help Everyone Link Interests and Expertise (HELIX)

HELIX is an application created by people that believe interdisciplinary collaboration leads to innovative solutions. We recognized that faculty members and students have a variety of expertise and interests. Our goal was to connect these diverse individuals and Help Everyone Link Interests and Expertise: HELIX. Users can create or browse through research projects, or simply search for individuals with a particular interest or expertise.

Product Owner

I served as Product Owner (Agile) for this flagship product. Conducted daily standups, crafted feature requirements, interfaced with stakeholders, directed product design, and personally delivered the product to market.
